What is the current understanding of the causes and mechanisms of climate
change, and what can individuals and governments do to mitigate its effects?
--
Climate change is a major global issue that has received significant attention
in recent years. It is the result of human activity, including the burning of
fossil fuels, deforestation, and other land-use changes, which release large
amounts of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere. These gases trap heat and cause
the Earth's temperature to rise, leading to a range of environmental and
societal impacts.
